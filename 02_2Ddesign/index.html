CAD Practice: Calipers

In making the Calipers I learned how to transition from Onshape to Fusion, learning the different functions and the similarities and differences between the programs. This was especially true for the assembly part as creating joints were very different from how they worked in Onshape. I spent a lot of time struggling on why I could not put joints on both my parts, in the end a classmate helped me reavling that to create joints, parts had to be exported from two different files.
